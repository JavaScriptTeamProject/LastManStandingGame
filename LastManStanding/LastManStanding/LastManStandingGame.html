To move use the arrows or WASD. To shoot use the left mouse click. To pause the game and go back to the menu, press "P".

There once was a very lonely wizard. He knew his chances of finding his half were not so good, so he decided to boost them up, by going to a parallel universe in the future, where men are significantly fewer. A bit after his arrival he saw that this was a huge mistake, because a group of space amazons are abducting all men, leaving him in a difficult and dangerous situtation, between women in strong need of male company.
